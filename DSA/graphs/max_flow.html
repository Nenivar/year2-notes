To prove a time bound:

  1. Each iteration takes O(E)O(E)O(E) time, where EEE is the number of edges in the network
  2. The flow value increases by at least one unit in each iteration
  3. The number of iterations is at most ∣f∗∣\vert f *\vert∣f∗∣, where ∣f∗∣\vert f*\vert∣f∗∣ is the maximum flow value
