Minimum Spanning Trees

In a connected, undirected graph GGG, a spanning tree is a subgraph TTT s.t:

  • every vertex v∈Vv\in Vv∈V is in TTT, and TTT is a tree (no cycles)
  • the weight of a spanning tree is the sum of the weights of its edges

TTT is a minimum spanning tree if no other spanning tree has a lower weight
